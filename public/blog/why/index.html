Our Why

May 10, 2025

Technology should feel like an extension of you. We believe the future belongs to more natural, human ways of interacting with the systems around us.

Today's tech often prioritizes profits over people. Mobile apps are constantly optimizing for engagement and fighting for your attention.

We're building a world where interacting with computers feels less like using a tool and more like having a conversation.

If you feel strongly about the future of human-computer interaction, feel free to reach out. We'd love to hear your thoughts.
